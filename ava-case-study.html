AVA Festival

About The Project

As part of my application I had been asked to pick out of three options to design for my application. The options were; design a t-shirt which would be on sale at AVA 2019, design a stage set up for AVA 2019 or design a social media campaign. I chose the social media campaign to work on as I thought it gave me the most room to be creative.

The Situation

Before sketching or even going onto Illustrator I decided to do some research into their previous social media posts. From my research I was able to construct an idea of their aesthetic and brand guidelines which I wanted to follow through into my own work. Once I had constructed my research into their previous social media campaigns, I began sketching layouts of different features of the campaign. I decided I would design an annoucement poster, set lists poster, a cover photo for the events page and two ticket warning posts.
